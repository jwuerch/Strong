Meet Dr. Strong

Dr. Sharen Strong is committed to providing the most advanced, comfortable dentistry for you and your family, ensuring a lifetime of beautiful smiles. A graduate of the Oregon Health and Science University School of Dentistry, Dr. Strong has also attended postdoctoral studies specializing in connective tissue grafting and dental implants. With more than twice the required continuing education, It is her goal to provide genuine, quality care in a relaxed and fun environment.

Dr. Strong and her husband enjoy spending their time with their children and grandchildren. In her free time, Dr. Strong enjoys surfing, cycling, and hiking.

Dr. Strong is a member of the American Academy of Implant Dentistry, the American Dental Association, the Academy of General Dentistry, and the South Coast Dental Association.

Dan

Dan is our Practice Manager. Although most of our patients have met him, Dan usually works "behind the scenes" to make sure things run smoothly. He works closely with the staff, takes photos of patient to document "before and after" improvement, and writes our newsletters and ads.

Personal:

Dan has college degrees in physics and English, and earned a Master's degree in marine biology. Before he came to our office, he worked as a high school teacher, marine biology researcher, research chemist and building inspector. Dan is married to Dr. Strong. They live in the Charleston area with two cats and two dogs. Surfing, biking and hiking are a big part of his life outside the office, as well as working around their home.

What I like best about my job:

Dan loves the changing challenges of each day. "We have a remarkable staff! We work together well, support one another, and have common goals in helping patients reach the highest level of health. We love helping our patients, and it really shows!"
